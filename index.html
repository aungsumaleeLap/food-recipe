Sweet Patato and Kale Bowl

This loaded veggie bowl gets a touch sweet potatoes. Enjoy as a snack. Use it to top tacos, stir into whole-grain salads, or top roasted broccoli.

Ingredients

For the sweet potato

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup milk

For the kale

  • 1 tablespoon bacon fat
  • 3 cups chopped kale
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
